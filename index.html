Research

Urban Crosswalk Perception

Multi season panoramic video aligned to RTK GNSS. Detection with YOLOv9e, tracking with DeepSORT, projection to world frame via calibrated homographies, and trajectory stitching across camera splits. Metrics include IDF1, HOTA, and track level precision–recall.

Panoramic vision RTK GNSS YOLOv9e DeepSORT

GNSS‑Denied Navigation

Self supervised visual–inertial odometry in CARLA and on a 1/10 scale vehicle. Drift and uncertainty measured with ATE and RPE, designed so planners can consume calibrated posteriors.

VIO CARLA Uncertainty

1/10 Scale Autonomy

Closed loop path following with disciplined logging and quick ablations. Fast hardware loops that keep ideas honest.

ROS C++ Python
